WHAT IS INTEGRATED AGRICULTURE?

Integrated agriculture is the practice of using a farm with both livestock and crops in order to be environmentally friendly while still maintaning a high income for farmers.

Why should you pick integrated farming?

Here's why you should pick integrated agriculture for your farm!
Integrated farming is a good choice for your farm as it is very sustainable.
Integrated farming's use of livestock and crops insures that you will never have to pay for fertilizer again due to using animal manure as fertilizer!
It also brings maintanence costs down for your farm and increases soil fertility.
